Eminence Chemical Industries Ltd. is a renowned agro based chemical company

Eminence Chemical Industries Ltd. is a renowned agro based chemical company operating its business since 2005. After its establishment it does not have to look back rather moving forward. It has been successfully spreading its business all over the country. It imports various kinds of herbicide, insecticide, fungicide and yield booster from countries like China, Germany, Argentina etc. and distributes all over the country. Eminence Chemical Industries Ltd. has always been with many renowned tea companies of Bangladesh serving through supplying high quality products since the beginning. Because of the high quality products and quick services, Eminence Chemical Industries Ltd. has succeeded to occupy a space in almost every tea company in Bangladesh. It has captured approximately 40% of the tea pesticide market and it is growing every year. Besides capturing the major market share of tea industries it has successfully captured the field crop markets all over the country as well. Since the beginning Eminence Chemical Industries Ltd. has been serving the farmers throughout their whole cultivation process by providing necessary fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides, yield boosters and most importantly information for their better production as well as well being.
