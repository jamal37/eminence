About Eminence Chemical

Eminence Chemical Industries Ltd. is a renowned agro based chemical company operating its business since 2005. After its establishment it does not have to look back rather moving forward. It has been successfully spreading its business all over the country. It imports various kinds of herbicide, insecticide, fungicide and yield booster from countries like China, Germany, Argentina etc. and distributes all over the country.
